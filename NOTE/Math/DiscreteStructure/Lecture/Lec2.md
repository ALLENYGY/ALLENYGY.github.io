# Logic and Proofs- Propositional Equivalence

## Tautology

* Tautology(恒真)
* contradiction(矛盾)
* contingency(偶然)

## Logical Equivalence

* Two propositions (P,Q) are equivalent if the proposition $$P≡Q$$
  * If two propositions are equivalent, they must have the same truth table.
  
## Laws

* Identity(同一) laws  
  * $p\wedge T \equiv p$
  * $p\vee F \equiv p$
* Domination(支配) laws
  * $p \vee T \equiv T$
  * $p \wedge F \equiv F$
* Idempotent (幂等) laws
  * $p\vee p\equiv p$
  * $p\wedge p\equiv p$
* Double negation laws
  * $\neg(\neg p)\equiv p$
