# Logical Equivalence

  • Two propositions (P,Q) are equivalent if the proposition $$P≡Q$$
    • If two propositions are equivalent, they must have the same truth table.

# Laws

  • Identity(同一) laws
    • p∧T≡pp\wedge T \equiv pp∧T≡p
    • p∨F≡pp\vee F \equiv pp∨F≡p
  • Domination(支配) laws
    • p∨T≡Tp \vee T \equiv Tp∨T≡T
    • p∧F≡Fp \wedge F \equiv Fp∧F≡F
  • Idempotent (幂等) laws
    • p∨p≡pp\vee p\equiv pp∨p≡p
    • p∧p≡pp\wedge p\equiv pp∧p≡p
  • Double negation laws
    • ¬(¬p)≡p\neg(\neg p)\equiv p¬(¬p)≡p
